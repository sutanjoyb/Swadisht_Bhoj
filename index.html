Our Specialities

A classic Indian delicacy made with fragrant basmati rice, tender marinated chicken, and a blend of aromatic spices, slow-cooked to perfection. Served with cooling raita, our Chicken Biryani offers rich flavor, authentic taste, and a satisfying meal experience.

Chicken Kabab is a classic delicacy made from tender chicken pieces marinated in traditional Indian spices and herbs, then perfectly grilled to achieve a smoky aroma and juicy texture. Served hot and fresh, it offers a rich, bold flavor that makes it a favorite starter and a signature speciality at Heritage Kitchen.

Experience the authentic, robust flavors of North India with Heritage Kitchen's signature Kadhai Paneer. Tender paneer cubes are tossed with crunchy bell peppers and onions in a rich tomato gravy, infused with freshly ground, aromatic spices.

About Us

Heritage Kitchen is a celebration of authentic Indian flavours and timeless culinary traditions. Founded in 2010, the restaurant began as a passion-driven venture rooted in heritage, quality, and warm hospitality. Today, it continues to serve soulful recipes crafted with care and honesty.
