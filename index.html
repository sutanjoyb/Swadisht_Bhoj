Our Specialities

A classic Indian delicacy made with fragrant basmati rice, tender marinated chicken, and a blend of aromatic spices, slow-cooked to perfection. Served with cooling raita, our Chicken Biryani offers rich flavor, authentic taste, and a satisfying meal experience.

Chicken Kabab is a classic delicacy made from tender chicken pieces marinated in traditional Indian spices and herbs, then perfectly grilled to achieve a smoky aroma and juicy texture. Served hot and fresh, it offers a rich, bold flavor that makes it a favorite starter and a signature speciality at Swadisht Bhoj.

Experience the authentic, robust flavors of North India with Swadisht Bhoj's signature Kadhai Paneer. Tender paneer cubes are tossed with crunchy bell peppers and onions in a rich tomato gravy, infused with freshly ground, aromatic spices.
